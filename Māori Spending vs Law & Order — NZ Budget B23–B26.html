The sharpest divergence in the coalition's fiscal record. Across Budgets 2024–2026, targeted Māori spending has been cut by approximately 74% while combined Law & Order spending has risen by approximately 73%. These lines move in precisely opposite directions. The operating allowance of $2.1B for Budget 2026 leaves no fiscal space for restoration of Māori-specific programmes absent a fundamental change in the revenue architecture.
