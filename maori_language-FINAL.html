Key Changes

  • B24: Aotearoa Reorua (Bilingual Towns & Cities) cut 64% — from $1.1M to $400K/yr
  • B24: Matariki public holiday funding cut 45% — from $5.5M to $3M
  • B24: Te reo Māori bilingual signage funding cut
  • B25: Aotearoa Reorua scrapped entirely ($1.6M returned to Crown)
  • B25: Māori Language Funding to Support Provision reprioritised
  • B25: Climate Resilience for Māori fund cut 33% to avg $2.6M/yr
  • B26: No language restoration signals

Te Taura Whiri i te Reo Māori + Aotearoa Reorua programme + Matariki public holiday fund + te reo signage funding. Excludes broadcasting (Te Māngai Pāho, Māori TV — separate media appropriations). B23: ~$45M. B24: Aotearoa Reorua cut 64% to $400K/yr (Spinoff confirmed); Matariki cut from $5.5M to $3M (45% cut); te reo signage funding cut. B25: Aotearoa Reorua scrapped entirely ($1.6M returned); remaining Māori Language Funding to Support Provision reprioritised. Source: Spinoff, RNZ, budget.govt.nz Vote 7.

B26 (PROJECTED): Aotearoa Reorua (Bilingual Towns) scrapped in B25. Te reo signage funding removed in B24. Matariki public holiday funding cut 45% in B24. No B26 restoration signals for any of these programmes.
Source: budget.govt.nz Vote documents · Treasury Estimates of Appropriations · RNZ · The Spinoff · Waatea News
